# Astra canonical credit cards (demo product knowledge)

## HDFC Bank — Swiggy Credit Card (last4 2109)

Best for: Swiggy, Zomato, and other food-delivery apps. This co-brand is designed so food-delivery spend earns the strongest rewards on the platform compared with generic spend on the same issuer’s other cards.

Typical positioning: elevated cashback or points on Swiggy/Zomato (exact rates, caps, and exclusions are in the issuer’s terms and change over time).

Avoid relying on this card as the primary choice for international travel, forex, or large offline purchases where the merchant category is not food delivery.

When answering users: combine this with their actual debits — if Swiggy spend is already on this card, usage is usually optimal for that category.

## Federal Bank — Swiggy Credit Card (last4 8765)

Best for: Food delivery (Swiggy, Zomato) and similar online food orders where the co-brand applies.

Typical positioning: Swiggy-focused rewards; compare with HDFC Swiggy in the same wallet using real spend and limits, not assumptions.

Avoid: treating it as a travel or lounge card unless the product docs explicitly say so.

---

## SBI — Cashback Credit Card (last4 4321)

Best for: General online shopping, Amazon-style e-commerce, and broad everyday spend where flat cashback applies.

Typical positioning: cashback on eligible online and sometimes offline categories; rates vary by merchant and campaign.

For Swiggy specifically: prefer whichever Swiggy co-brand (HDFC or Federal) actually shows food-delivery spend in the user’s data — SBI Cashback is stronger when Amazon or broad shopping dominates.
